## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if my results are positive?
A: Results remain in your Private Inbox. You are NOT required to verify your profile. Your information stays completely private. We recommend consulting with a healthcare provider.

### Q: Can I use results from my own doctor?
A: Yes, you can upload documentation from any CLIA-certified laboratory through our Certifications tab.

### Q: How long is my verification valid?
A: Standard verification is valid for 60 days. Annual members should re-verify every 90 days for best practices.

### Q: Who can see my verification status?
A: Only people you share your QR code with, and only for a limited time window (3 minutes viewing access).

### Q: What if I disagree with my results?
A: Contact our support team. We can facilitate re-testing or connect you with lab customer service.
